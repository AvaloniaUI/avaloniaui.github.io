Interface IReactiveList<T>

IReactiveList of T represents a list that can notify when its contents are changed (either items are added/removed, or the object itself changes).

It is important to implement the Changing/Changed from IReactiveNotifyPropertyChanged semantically as "Fire when anything in the collection or any of its items have changed, in any way".
